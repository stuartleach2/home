Yorkshire Dales Waterfalls

I had a phase of travelling around the Yorkshire Dales in 2020 looking for waterfalls to photpgraph with a Nikon D3500 and a tripod. There is a handy pocket guide Walks to the Best Waterfalls in the Yorkshire Dales by Neil Coates. I also found a very helpful introduction to shooting waterfalls by Justin Reznick on Linkedin Learning.

Scale Haw Force near Hebden in North Yorkshire

Scalebar Force, a 40ft waterfall in Scalebar Woods above Skipton.

West Burton Falls, aka Cauldron Falls, was painted by Turner in 1816.

Mill Gill Force is a nice walk from the village of Askrigg in Wensleydale..

Lower Falls at Aysgarth. A tourist attraction for over 200 years. Wordsworth visited them, Turner sketched them and they featured in Robin Hood: Prince of Thieves.

Catrigg Force near Stainforth, just north of Settle. My first waterfall (you can tell).

Hengjanefossen waterfall in Lysefjorden, Norway. Not Yorkshire I know, but I couldn't end with those Catrigg Force shots and it is pretty.
