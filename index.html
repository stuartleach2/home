About this site

A photography site with a slightly unusual beginning

This place started on an iPhone, early in the morning, from a sun lounger in the hills above Agia Effimia in Kefalonia. The AI-assisted origin story is still part of it — but the reason for keeping it going is simpler: photographs, places, family memories, and curiosity.

I hope the site feels personal rather than polished to perfection: a home for landscapes, waterfalls, northern skies and the stories that gather around them.
