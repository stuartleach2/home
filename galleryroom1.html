Southwold Harbour B&W

During our trip to Southwold harbour, I tried to capture the aged, weathered beauty of the boats and huts in black and white but gave in to a little colour in the edit. Feeling uninspired, I have been leaving the camera in the boot of the car lately, using instead my iPhone 14 hence the grain and Snapseed, which is a great little app. After exploring and photographing, we enjoyed a delightful lunch of lobster roll and mussels at Le Roc restaurant.
