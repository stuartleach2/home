Lapland Aurora (Shot on iPhone)

February 2026 by Stuart Leach

The first thing I noticed wasn’t colour — it was motion. A slow, silent shifting in the sky that made everything else feel briefly irrelevant: the cold, the waiting, the people.

These are iPhone frames from Akaslompolo / Ylläs. I wasn’t trying to “make” dramatic images — I just wanted them to feel like it felt: dark, vast, and quietly alive.

A small “how I shot it” note

  • Night Mode on, braced or tripod when possible
  • Keep edits restrained (avoid crushing the blacks)
  • Let some darkness stay dark — it makes the sky feel bigger

There’s something humbling about it. You don’t feel like you’ve captured anything. You feel like you’ve been allowed to witness it — briefly — before it slips away again.

– Stuart Leach
